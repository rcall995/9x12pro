How to Generate Facing Slips

Step-by-step guide for creating USPS facing slips

1

Get Your Route Data from USPS

Go to USPS EDDM Route Selection and select your target routes by checking the boxes.

2

Import Routes (Choose One Method)

Bookmarklet: Drag the purple "Grab USPS Routes" button to your bookmarks bar. Click it on USPS, then paste here.

Manual Entry: Enter ZIP once, then list routes as "C001, 517" (one per line).

One at a Time: Use the form fields to add routes individually.

3

Set Mailing Details

Enter your mailing date and company name (optional). Bundle size defaults to 100 pieces (USPS recommended).

4

Generate & Print

Click "Generate Facing Slips" to preview, then "Print All Slips". Place one slip on top of each bundle.

üí° Tip: USPS requires bundles of 50-100 pieces, max 6" high. The tool automatically calculates how many bundles you need and creates a facing slip for each one.
