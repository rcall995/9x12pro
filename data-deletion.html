Data Deletion Instructions

How to delete your 9x12 Pro account and data

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including prospects, campaigns, and financial records will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, profile)
  • All prospect and client data
  • Postcard designs and campaigns
  • Sales pipeline and deal tracking data
  • Financial records and revenue tracking
  • All saved searches and cached data
  • Message templates and interaction history

Method 1: Delete Account Within the App

  1. Log in to your 9x12 Pro account
  2. Click on your Profile/Settings icon (top-right corner)
  3. Navigate to Account Settings
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion by entering your password
  6. Click "Permanently Delete My Account"

Data Retention After Deletion

Immediately deleted:

  • Your ability to log in
  • All personal data from active databases
  • All business data (prospects, campaigns, financials)

Retained for up to 30 days:

  • Database backups (for disaster recovery)
  • Server logs (for security purposes)

Retained permanently (if required by law):

  • Financial transaction records (tax compliance)
  • Legal dispute documentation
