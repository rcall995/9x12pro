Pro Tips from the Pros

  • Don't give up after email 1. Most responses come from emails 2-4.
  • Reply to your own thread. Keep subject line as "Re: ..." to stay in their inbox.
  • Time your sends. Tuesday-Thursday mornings work best. Avoid Mondays and Fridays.
  • The "breakup email" works. Email 4 often gets the most responses!
  • Call after email 4. "Hey, I sent a few emails - wanted to make sure I'm a real person!"

Objection Handlers

Quick responses to common pushback. Copy and customize as needed.

"I don't have the budget right now"

Totally understand - timing is everything. For what it's worth, this is a fraction of what solo mailers cost since we split it between businesses. But I'll keep you in mind for a future card when timing is better. Would that work?

"Direct mail doesn't work"

I hear that a lot, and honestly some direct mail doesn't work. The difference here is saturation - we're hitting EVERY home in [ZIP], not a random list. And you're the only [CATEGORY] on the card, so there's no competition for attention. Most of our advertisers see the best results after 2-3 mailings when people start recognizing their name.

"I just do word of mouth / referrals"

Word of mouth is the best! This actually helps with that - when your name is in front of [HOME_COUNT] households, people remember you when their neighbor asks for a recommendation. It's like priming the pump for referrals.

"I already do Facebook/Google ads"

Those are great for people actively searching! This hits a different audience - the people who WOULD use a [CATEGORY] but aren't actively looking yet. Plus, direct mail has a 4.4% response rate vs about 0.6% for digital. Most successful businesses do both.

"I'm too busy / have too much work already"

That's a great problem to have! A lot of our advertisers use these mailings to stay visible during their slow season, or to be picky about jobs. When you're in front of [HOME_COUNT] homes regularly, you can choose the jobs you want instead of taking everything that comes in.

"Send me some info and I'll think about it"

Absolutely! What's the best email? [GET EMAIL] I'll send that right over. Just so I know how to follow up - are you looking at this for the upcoming mailing or more for down the road?

"How do I know this actually works?"

Great question. We've had [X] businesses advertise with us so far, and many of them renew month after month because they see results. I can put you in touch with a current advertiser if you want to hear it directly from them. Or you could try one mailing and see for yourself - no long-term commitment required.
